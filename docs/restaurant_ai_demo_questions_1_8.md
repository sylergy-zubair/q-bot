# Restaurant AI Demo Questions (Multi-Store)

## 1. Revenue & Performance (Owner / Director)
- How has total revenue changed month-on-month across all stores?
- Which store is underperforming compared to the chain average?
- Show me revenue growth by store over the last 90 days.
- Which stores are declining despite stable footfall?
- What percentage of revenue comes from dine-in vs delivery?

**Follow-ups**
- Why did revenue dip in July?
- Break that down by channel.

---

## 2. Store Comparison (Multi-site Focus)
- Compare Brixton Village vs Camden Lock Market revenue and order volume.
- Which store has the highest average order value (AOV)?
- Which store sells the most items per order?
- Rank stores by revenue per order.
- Which stores rely too heavily on delivery platforms?

**Follow-ups**
- What are they selling differently?
- Show menu mix differences between top and bottom stores.

---

## 3. Menu Engineering (Profit Levers)
- Which menu items generate high revenue but low order volume?
- Which best-selling items have the lowest margins?
- Which products should be promoted to increase AOV?
- Which items are popular but underpriced?
- Which items are declining in popularity?

**Killer Question**
- Which product should we push next month to maximise revenue?

**Follow-up**
- Show me the data behind that.

---

## 4. Marketing & Promotions
- Which items would benefit most from a 10% promotion?
- What menu items convert well on delivery platforms but not in-store?
- Which items perform best on Just Eat vs Deliveroo?
- Which products are ordered together most often?
- What should we bundle to increase basket size?

**Follow-up**
- Create a promo idea based on that insight.

---

## 5. Channel Optimisation (Delivery vs Dine-in)
- How much revenue do we lose to delivery commissions?
- Which stores are over-dependent on Just Eat?
- Which items perform poorly on delivery?
- Which menu items should be delivery-only?
- What happens to revenue if Deliveroo drops 10% next month?

---

## 6. Operational & Cost Signals
- Which items sell a lot but contribute little revenue?
- Which categories should be simplified?
- Which starters are underperforming across all stores?
- Are drinks being under-sold compared to mains?
- Which stores sell fewer add-ons per order?

**Follow-up**
- What operational change would you suggest?

---

## 7. Trends & Seasonality
- What seasonal trends can we see across the last year?
- Which items spike during winter vs summer?
- Are customers ordering fewer items per order over time?
- How does weekend behaviour differ from weekdays?
- Which store benefits most from peak periods?

---

## 8. Executive-Level Insights (The “So What?”)
- If we could only focus on three things next month, what should they be?
- Where are we leaking revenue?
- What single change would increase revenue fastest?
- Which store needs immediate attention?
- What would you do if this was your business?
